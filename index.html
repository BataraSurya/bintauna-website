About

A software company from Bintauna, North Sulawesi.

bintauna.io builds digital systems and software products across a wide range of domains: public-sector workflows, creative AI platforms, medical software concepts, design integrations, and experimental tools for the next generation of human-computer interaction.

Our work is driven by a simple belief: useful software can be built anywhere, and serious technology does not have to emerge only from major capitals or global centers. We are rooted in Bintauna, and we treat that identity as part of our strength.

Why Bintauna Matters

We are building from Bintauna, not despite it.

Our location shapes how we think: stay practical, stay adaptive, and build systems that matter. We believe important software can come from places outside the usual maps of the tech industry.

This geographic reality is a strength, not a footnote. bintauna.io stands for the idea that meaningful software can be designed and shipped from anywhere — including places the global tech narrative rarely pays attention to.

Our Approach
I

Build useful systems

We value systems that work in practice, not just in slides, demos, or theory. Progress comes from making, testing, refining, and releasing.
